Coconute Shell Charcoal Coconut shell charcoal briquettes are briquettes made from coconut shells as a substitute for charcoal and coal.

Coco Fiber Coir, also known as coconut fiber, is a natural fiber obtained from the outer skin of coconuts.
Coconut Sugar Coconut sugar is a natural sweetener made from coconut sap, which is the sweet liquid extracted from coconut flowers.
